About

Little Brahma, is a design arm of Hashworks which started with a 4 member team in 2016 to bridge the gap between technology and end users.

We evolved over years and have been able to deliver enterprise level design solutions. We have worked with organization in more than 10+ domains. In 2017, our website received an award for “Site of the Day”.

We believe in processes

Our designers practice lean + design thinking. We work hard to meet the business goals with a little blend of playfulness to it. Thinkers & Doers work hand-in-hand here, our design principles are authentic and evolved over years of learning & repeating the success.

We believe in being partners and not an ‘agency’

We collaborate and believe in being the partner in our clients growth. We take the ownership and feel accountable. We don’t work on all the projects that come our way, we select our clients. We always look for value addition and what kind of people we will be working with.

We like individuality

We take pride in our work and who we are. We respect the craftsmanship and individual ideas that each of the stakeholders bring to the table. We adapt and learn to deliver and scale faster.

We don’t work in Silos

We are researchers, designers, technologists and startup enthusiasts who partner with organisations at different stages and devise solutions in diverse sectors. We collaborate to innovate and scale faster.
